Statement of purpose

I’m Allwin Williams from the farmlands of Lalgudi on the outskirts of Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. I chose to pursue Information Technology to create digital solutions that enable and assist people in their daily lives. I continue to be pursuing the same goal now in interaction design to imagining delightful digital experiences with a better understanding of people. I’m looking forward to being associated with a dynamic and innovative organization where I can apply my skills to create meaningful and elegant digital experiences and in the process grow myself along with the organization. I am curious about integrating my design skills with technical knowledge to deliver solutions that are more cohesive in nature keeping in mind the human factors and experience as well as focusing on making the solutions a part of and seamlessly integrated with the larger system, society, and ultimately our planet.
