The low-value small business owners in India are neglected by the traditional banking system and financial institutions. I spoke to them about their financial aspirations and documented what I learnt.


I published my findings at D91 Labs, an open-source research initiative aimed at financial inclusivity in India


Link to the research article

Based on my understanding of the small and street businesses and their aspirations, I worked on a digital service that can enable them to meet their financial aspirations.
