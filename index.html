A brief history of me

I started my journey from a small agricultural town called Lalgudi in the district of Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. My father does agricultural and co-owns a small bakery along with his friends while my mom takes care of our home. I have an elder brother who is currently a product developer who introduced me to computers for which I am really grateful. I have always been fasinated about how technology impacts people's life from my school days. I decided to study a technical course in college and so I did my undergrad in Information Technology from Coimbatore Institute of Technology, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. After which, I started pursuing graduate degree in Interaction Design from National Institute of Design, Bengaluru, Karnataka.

I like watching crime thrillers while having a plate of biryani along with some coke, especially when it's raining outside. I also like listening to AR Rahman, and playing the piano.
